# Who are we?

We are a group of Faculty, Academic Professional, Classified Employees, and Students who are concerned about transparency in Oregon higher education.

# What do we do?

We research issues impacting our communities and we publish our findings. We verify everything we publish to a high standard.

# Why do we do it?

We care deeply about our students and our community. Our students deserve a top notch education with leadership who we can point to as examplary citizens and leaders.
